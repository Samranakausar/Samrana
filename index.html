Myself Samrana Kausar, I'm a business coach an Entrepreneur & the founder of Fortunate Womens Circle (FWC)... At the age of 21, I have earned more than 22 lakh rupees through this platform and presently I'm a 7 figure earner at the age of 21. There are over 500+ females who have started working with me, and I help them become financially independent through digital assistance. When I started my journey with IDP, before that, I had been a victim of fraud in a data entry job. I had a great need for online work because I had to financially support my family, and as a hijabi girl, it was impossible for me to work offline, especially since I had only completed high school.When I decided to join IDP, my father was in Mumbai for his business purposes, but suddenly he fell ill and was admitted to Mumbai's J.J. Hospital. Then my mother and brother travelled from Kannauj to Mumbai, but there was no one in my family to financially support them. So, I arranged a phone call with my sister to start my IDP journey. Initially, some people accused me of fraud or wasting their time, but I didn't budge and responded to them with my success. I started my IDP journey on November 20, 2022, and after a lot of hard work, received my first payout of ₹7,600 credited to my account on January 21, 2023. Then, within two months, I earned ₹2 lakhs and qualified for an event called Conclave or Astitva organized by the company. Throughout the year, I created earnings exceeding ₹18 lakhs and helped more than 500 women become financially independent. Alhamdulillah... deteriorated, and there was no one else to provide financial support. I managed to arrange a phone call with my sister, but some people wrongly accused me. However, I didn't let their words deter me. Instead, I responded with my success. Today, they themselves have become part of the same business, thanks to be to Allah Since then, my life has completely changed 360 degrees. Alhamdulillah As I mentioned, I started a startup on this platform on November 20, 2022, and within just one year, *I took on the responsibility of my elder sister's wedding. Her wedding took place on October 30, 2023, and I managed all the expenses including jewelry and dowry. Thanks to the grace of God, in today's era where girls' weddings often don't happen due to the lack of dowry, I managed to accomplish all of this at the age of just 21 I am grateful to my Lord who made me capable and introduced me to this platform Now, I proudly want to say that every girl should be so independent that she can fulfill her own dreams as well as the dreams of her family."
